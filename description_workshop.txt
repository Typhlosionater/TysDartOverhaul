~Ty's Dart Overhaul ~

Ty's Dart Overhaul is a small content mod made to expand on the vanilla dart subclass by adding multiple new dartguns, dart ammunition types and dart sub-class accessories, with the goal of making darts more viable, fun and closer in line with bows and guns.

Contents:
 - 14 new dartguns.
 - 8 new dart ammunition types.
 - An infinite dart ammunition.
 - 3 new accessories
 - A shroomite headpiece variant for darts.
 - Some small changes to vanilla darts to make them work better.

In addition, all of these features can be enabled or disabled via the internal config to fit the mod to your liking.
